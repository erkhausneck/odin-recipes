Tabbouleh


Description

Tabbouleh, also transcribed tabouleh, tabbouli, tabouli, or taboulah, is a Levantine salad made mostly of finely chopped parsley, with tomatoes, mint, onion, soaked uncooked bulgur, and seasoned with olive oil, lemon juice, salt and sweet pepper. Some variations add lettuce, or use semolina instead of bulgur.

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 garlic clove, grated
  • ½ teaspoon sea salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 pinch cinnamon
  • 3 cups finely chopped curly parsley
  • 1 cup diced English cucumber
  • 1 cup cored and diced tomato
  • ⅔ cup cooked bulgur wheat
  • ⅓ cup finely chopped fresh mint
  • 2 scallions, chopped

Cooking Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, coriander, and cinnamon. Add the parsley, cucumber, tomato, bulgur, mint, and scallions and toss to combine.
  2. Season to taste and chill until ready to serve.
